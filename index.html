Application of Derivatives in Bioscience

Microbial Growth Velocity in Cocoa Fermentation Industry in Sabah

Bioscience Problem Explanation

In cocoa fermentation industries in Sabah, monitoring Lactobacillus growth is important to maintain cocoa bean quality. Using derivatives allows scientists to identify the exact rate of microbial growth at specific times. This helps plantation managers improve fermentation efficiency, reduce contamination risks, and ensure consistent cocoa quality.


Derivative analysis also supports precision agriculture and modern biotechnology systems by providing real-time microbial monitoring.

Mathematical Model

Microbial Growth Function:


f(x) = x² + 3x + 2


Derivative Function:


f'(x) = 2x + 3


The derivative represents the microbial growth velocity during the fermentation process.
